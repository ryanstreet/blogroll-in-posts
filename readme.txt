Easily add your blogroll or links list into your posts and pages!

== Description ==

Ever want to use your blogroll in your pages or posts?  Ever wanted to show a list of links in a category without editing your templates or using a sidebar widget?  Now you can! Easily add and customize a link list with this easy to use plugin.  Simply add the <code>[blogroll]</code> shortcode to your pages or posts and it will automatically convert into a full fledged blogroll!

== Installation ==


= Wordpress Installer = 
1. After you have downloaded your file, log into your WordPress Admin Section.
1. Go to Plugins-> Add New.
1. Go to the Upload Link.
1. Navigate to your file.
1. Click on the Upload Button.
1. After it is has installed, click on the Activation Link.

= Manual Installation =
1. Upload `blogroll-in-posts.php` plugin file to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
